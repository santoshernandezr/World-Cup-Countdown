France

The 2018 World Cup was held in Russia, a freezing country even during the summer time. In 2018 France was the team to look out for, they had the immerging talents such as Ousmane Dembele, Kylian Mbappe, N'golo Kante, and the list goes on. This was a reliable team that steamrolled through the tournament. They went undefeated through the group stage and went on to win the whole tournament against a surprising but fighting Croatia.
